# A Course-Grounded Teaching Assistant Built on Retrieval and the Model Context Protocol

- **Authors:** Petrova, I.; Sánchez-Vega, J.
- **Year:** 2025
- **Keywords:** education, teaching assistant, MCP, RAG, grounding

## Abstract

We describe an AI teaching assistant for an undergraduate machine learning course, grounded exclusively in course materials through a retrieval layer exposed as an MCP server. The assistant answers student questions with citations to lecture notes, flags questions it cannot ground, and escalates to instructors. Over one semester with 240 students, 71 percent of questions were resolved without instructor intervention, and hallucinated answers fell below 2 percent thanks to grounding checks. Students with access to the assistant scored 9 percent higher on problem sets, with the largest gains among students who rarely attended office hours.
