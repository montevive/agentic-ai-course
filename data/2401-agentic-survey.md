# A Survey of Agentic AI Architectures: From ReAct to Multi-Agent Systems

- **Authors:** Chen, L.; Martínez, R.; Okafor, D.
- **Year:** 2024
- **Keywords:** agentic AI, ReAct, survey, architectures, tool use

## Abstract

Agentic AI systems extend large language models with reasoning loops, tool use, and persistent memory. This survey reviews the evolution from single-shot prompting to the ReAct paradigm, covering perception-reasoning-action architectures, planning strategies, and the emergence of multi-agent coordination patterns. We analyze 87 systems published between 2022 and 2024, identifying three dominant architectural families: sequential pipelines, hierarchical supervisor-worker topologies, and consensus-based swarms. We conclude that robust tool integration and context management, rather than raw model capability, are the primary drivers of production reliability.
