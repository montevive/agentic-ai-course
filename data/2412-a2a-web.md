# Toward the Agentic Web: Agent-to-Agent Protocols and Open Coordination

- **Authors:** Mbeki, T.; Johansson, A.
- **Year:** 2025
- **Keywords:** A2A, agentic web, interoperability, delegation, multi-institution

## Abstract

As organizations deploy agents behind public endpoints, agent-to-agent (A2A) communication becomes the next interoperability frontier. We review emerging A2A proposals covering capability discovery, task delegation, and result verification between agents operated by different parties. In a pilot connecting research-group agents across three universities, A2A delegation completed cross-institutional literature searches 5.4 times faster than human-mediated exchange. Open problems remain in authentication, billing, and liability when autonomous agents transact on behalf of institutions.
