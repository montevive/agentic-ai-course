# Literature review over the course corpus

You are reviewing the mini-corpus of 12 fictional abstracts in `data/` (files `2401-*.md` to `2412-*.md`). Follow this procedure every time.

## Procedure

1. **Scope the question.** Restate the topic as one sentence before searching.
2. **Gather evidence.** Read every paper that plausibly touches the topic — never fewer than 3. Use the corpus search/fetch tools available to you.
3. **Extract, per paper**: the main claim, the method, and the headline metric (with its number). If a paper reports no number, say so explicitly.
4. **Synthesize.** Group papers by position (agree / disagree / orthogonal), then write the review using the template in [resources/review-template.md](resources/review-template.md).

## Rules

- Cite papers as `[2403]` (the 4-digit id), never by invented author names.
- Every quantitative claim must carry its number and its source id.
- If the corpus does not cover the topic, say "not covered by the corpus" — do not fill gaps from general knowledge.
- Maximum length: 300 words, excluding the references list.
